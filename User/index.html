For You...

  • You can book a new courier to be delivered easily in the "BOOK A COURIER" section. Just enter your pickup and drop location with other necessary details of the courier and within a few minutes your courier will be picked from your door step.
  • If you are unsure about booking your courier, no worries, CMS is there to help you out! Just go to the "CALCULATE ESTIMATED PRICE" section. There you can know the estimated price of your courier delivery without actually booking it. If you are satisfied with it then you can continue your booking from their itself instead of doing it again.
  • Want to know all your previous and current bookings? So easy! Just go to the "MY COURIERS" section. Here you can view the complete summary of your bookings. You can even track the status of your ongoing bookings. Cool ! Isn't it?
